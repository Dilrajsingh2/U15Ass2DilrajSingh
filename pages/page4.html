Meet Our Dedicated Team

At Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum – South Manchester, we are proud to be led by a team of experienced educators and charity trustees who are passionate about Slovak language, culture, and education. With backgrounds in both the Slovak and UK education systems, we are dedicated to providing high-quality learning experiences and fostering a strong Slovak community abroad. Each of us shares the same vision—to create a space where Slovak heritage is preserved, language is celebrated, and families feel supported in their bilingual journey.

Mgr. Mária Mušutová

Co-Founder | Chair of the Charity | Trustee | Curriculum & Slovak Language Teaching Developer for All Ages | Lead Teacher for AM Sessions

Background: Originally from the Orava region in Slovakia, Mária has lived in Manchester for 17 years and holds a PGCE degree in Modern Foreign Languages from Manchester Metropolitan University. She currently works as a Spanish and French teacher at a secondary school while also offering private Slovak lessons and translation services.

  • Creating projects that connect Slovak students abroad with schools in Slovakia
  • Introducing Slovak history in an engaging way through our programs
"I believe that Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum - South Manchester is more than just a school—it's a place where we can connect, support one another, and celebrate the traditions that are important for us and our children."

Ingrid Harandza

Co-Founder | Trustee | Early Years Educator | SEN Specialist | Creative & Cultural Programs

Background: Born in Rimavská Sobota, Ingrid has lived in Manchester for over 20 years. With a background in early years education, she is a qualified Early Years Educator and works as a teaching assistant in a UK primary school, focusing on young learners. Ingrid is also a Special Educational Needs (SEN) specialist, ensuring that children with different learning needs receive the support and inclusive education they deserve.

Her love for Slovak language, literature, and storytelling has been with her since childhood, and she has participated in poetry and prose competitions such as Hviezdoslavov Kubín.

  • Providing specialized support for SEN students
  • Teaching Slovak to young children through play, music, and movement
  • Leading creative, literary, and sports activities
  • Organizing cultural events that bring Slovak traditions to life
"I am excited that Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum - South Manchester allows me to combine my two passions—working with children and preserving our Slovak heritage—into a meaningful and supportive space for our community."

Zuzana Hanuliaková

Volunteer | Teaching Assistant | Social Media Coordinator

Background: Zuzana is a dedicated volunteer and mother of three, whose passion for Slovak culture and education shines through her work at SVC-SM. As a teaching assistant, she plays a vital role in supporting both teachers and students, ensuring that classes run smoothly and that each child gets the guidance they need.
