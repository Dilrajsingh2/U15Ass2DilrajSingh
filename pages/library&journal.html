📙 SVCSM Work Book – „Pracovný zošit“

We are proud to offer our very own Slovak language workbook, developed by our educators. It includes:

  • 📌 Grammar & Vocabulary Exercises
  • 📌 Cultural Activities
  • 📌 Creative Writing & Reading Tasks
  • 📌 Fun activities

📥 Get the Work Book

The workbook is available in two formats:

  • 💻 Free Online Version – Available for all students.
  • 🖨️ Downloadable & Printable Version – Available for purchase.
