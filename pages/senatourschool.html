Supporting Every Learner

At Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum – South Manchester, we believe that every child learns differently, and we want all our students to feel comfortable, supported, and engaged in their Slovak language journey. We know that some children may need a bit of extra help—whether it’s with language processing, attention, communication, or other learning challenges. That’s why we make sure our lessons are adaptable and inclusive so that everyone can learn at their own pace and in their own way.

How We Support Our Students

  • A Flexible and Supportive Learning Environment – We recognize that children have different learning needs, and we adapt our lessons to ensure everyone feels included and valued.
  • A Curriculum That Works for All – Our SEN specialist, Ingrid, helps shape our teaching approach so that students with different learning styles can follow along with ease.
  • Interactive and Hands-On Learning – We use games, visual materials, music, and movement to make Slovak learning fun and accessible for all children.
  • Patience, Encouragement, and Individual Attention – Our small class sizes allow us to give extra support to those who need it.

Events & Projects

At Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum – South Manchester, we believe that Slovak culture should be experienced, not just learned. That’s why we organise a variety of events for everyone—families, children, adults, and individuals who simply want to connect with Slovak heritage, make new friends, and enjoy great company.

Whether you’re a parent wanting to engage your child in Slovak traditions, a single professional looking to expand your social circle, or someone who just loves Slovak culture, our variety of events and workshops provides a welcoming space where people can enjoy themselves, have fun, eat traditional Slovak food, and build lasting connections.

Past Events

  • Fašiangový Karneval – A traditional Slovak carnival full of masks, costumes, music, and dancing. A fun opportunity for both kids and adults to enjoy the vibrant and playful atmosphere of this centuries-old tradition.
  • Christmas Workshop – A festive gathering where participants created handmade decorations, sang Slovak carols, and enjoyed mulled wine and traditional Christmas treats.
  • Open Day – A welcoming event for anyone curious about our school, projects, and cultural activities. Many attendees came to network, share experiences, and find new opportunities within the Slovak community.
  • Kids Disco 2024 – A high-energy event that brought children, parents, and community members together for an evening of music, dancing, and socializing.

Visit our event gallery to see highlights from our past events.

News & Leadership Activities

Beyond organising events, we actively represent the Slovak community, build new connections, and develop educational and cultural projects. This includes:

  • Attending Conferences & Networking Events – Engaging with Slovak professionals, cultural institutions, and educational leaders.
  • Meetings with Key Partners & Organizations – Strengthening collaborations with Slovak embassies, schools, and cultural programs.

How You Can Help

Until we finalise the structure of our library, we invite you to support this project in any way you can:

  • Donate Slovak Books: If you have Slovak children's books, novels, textbooks, or educational materials, we would love to add them to our collection.
  • Partner with Us: If you represent an organisation, business, or cultural institution interested in supporting our Slovak book section, let's collaborate!
  • Contribute Towards Library Costs: If you would like to help fund the creation of the library, donations are welcome. Contributions will go towards purchasing books, maintaining the collection, and covering logistical costs. (Navigate to our Payments Page for details.)

This is just the beginning of our library project! If you have ideas, resources, or want to get involved, we would love to hear from you. For book donations, partnerships, or sponsorship inquiries, please Contact Us.
