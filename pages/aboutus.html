Who We Are

Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum – South Manchester is a registered charity dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich Slovak language, traditions, and cultural heritage. Our mission is to provide high-quality Slovak language education while fostering a vibrant and inclusive community where individuals can celebrate and stay connected to their roots.

Our Purpose

Our charity exists to support Slovak-speaking families, children, and individuals who wish to maintain their language and culture while living abroad. However, we warmly welcome everyone—regardless of background—who has an interest in learning the Slovak language and exploring our beautiful customs and traditions.

What We Offer

  • A well-structured Saturday school offering Slovak language classes for children and adults.
  • Engaging cultural workshops focusing on traditional crafts, music, and folklore.
  • A variety of community events, including festive celebrations, storytelling sessions, and heritage days.
  • Social gatherings and support networks for Slovak families and individuals in South Manchester.

Join Our Community

By becoming a part of our charity, you will not only have the opportunity to learn and engage with Slovak culture, but you will also contribute to keeping our traditions alive for future generations. We invite you to take part in our educational programs, attend our vibrant events, and share in the joy of community spirit.
